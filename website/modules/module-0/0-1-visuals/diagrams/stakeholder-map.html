Stakeholder Engagement Strategy

Executive Sponsors

What they care about: Revenue protection, competitive advantage, customer satisfaction scores.
How to engage them: Monthly business impact dashboards, quarterly strategic reviews.
Success Metrics: Uptime %, revenue protected, customer satisfaction improvements.

Product & Engineering Leaders

What they care about: Development velocity, team productivity, technical excellence.
How to engage them: Weekly reliability reviews, sprint planning integration, capacity forecasting.
Success Metrics: Deployment frequency, lead time, team satisfaction scores.

Individual Contributors

What they care about: Reduced on-call burden, faster problem resolution, career development.
How to engage them: SRE training programs, mentoring, automation tools.
Success Metrics: On-call load reduction, incident resolution time, skill development.
