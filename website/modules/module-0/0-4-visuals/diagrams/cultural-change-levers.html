Cultural Change Levers

1. Leadership Modeling

  • Executives asking 'What's the reliability impact?'
  • Leadership sharing SRE success stories
  • Investment decisions considering reliability

2. Performance Integration

  • Performance reviews include reliability contribution
  • Team goals include SRE success metrics
  • Promotion criteria value reliability expertise

3. Recognition Systems

  • Celebrating reliability achievements and learning
  • Monthly reliability hero recognition
  • Annual reliability excellence awards

4. Communication Rhythms

  • Weekly reliability updates in all-hands
  • Monthly cross-functional reliability reviews
  • Quarterly organizational learning sessions

5. Training Investment

  • Continuous SRE skill development
  • Cross-functional reliability education
  • External conference and training investment
