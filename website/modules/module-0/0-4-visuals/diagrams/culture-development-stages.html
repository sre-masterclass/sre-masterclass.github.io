Four-Stage Culture Development

Stage 1: Awareness Building (Months 1-3)

Initiatives

  • SRE Education & Training Programs
  • Success Story Communication
  • Early Adopter Community Building

Success Metrics

  • 80% of engineering staff complete SRE fundamentals training
  • 5+ documented success stories
  • Active SRE community of practice with 50+ participants

Stage 2: Practice Adoption (Months 4-9)

Expansion

  • Cross-Team SRE Practice Implementation
  • Collaboration Pattern Establishment
  • Measurement & Feedback System Development

Success Metrics

  • All engineering teams have defined SLOs and error budgets
  • Cross-functional incident response time improved by 50%
  • Regular SRE practice reviews in all team retrospectives

Stage 3: Cultural Integration (Months 10-18)

Deepening

  • Organization-Wide SRE Principle Adoption
  • Leadership Behavior Modeling
  • Performance System Integration

Success Metrics

  • Product decisions routinely consider reliability impact
  • Leadership regularly references reliability in communications
  • Individual performance reviews include SRE contribution

Stage 4: Continuous Evolution (Months 18+)

Achievement

  • Self-Sustaining Improvement Culture
  • Innovation & Experimentation Mindset
  • Industry Leadership & Knowledge Sharing

Success Metrics

  • Teams independently develop and share reliability innovations
  • Industry conference speaking and thought leadership
  • External recognition for reliability excellence
