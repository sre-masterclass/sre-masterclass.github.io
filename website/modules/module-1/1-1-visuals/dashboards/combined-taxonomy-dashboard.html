Layered Monitoring Strategy

Layer 1: Foundation Monitoring (USE-based)

Monitors infrastructure health and capacity, detects hardware failures, and optimizes resources.

Responsibility: Platform Team

Layer 2: Service Monitoring (RED-based)

Tracks application performance, service reliability, and inter-service dependencies.

Responsibility: Development Team

Layer 3: Experience Monitoring (Golden Signals-based)

Focuses on user journey reliability, business impact, and customer satisfaction.

Responsibility: Product Team
