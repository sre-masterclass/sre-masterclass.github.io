**Q: "How do you get executive buy-in when they don't understand the technical details?"**
A: "Focus on business outcomes, not technical features. Use the ROI framework and speak their language: revenue, costs, competitive advantage, and risk mitigation. The technical details come later, after you have strategic alignment."

**Q: "What if our organization is too small for dedicated [SRE capability] roles?"**
A: "Start with the embedded model and focus on building [capability] as a skill set rather than dedicated roles. Many successful small organizations have [SRE practitioners] who wear multiple hats. The principles scale down effectively."

**Q: "How long does organizational transformation typically take?"**
A: "Phase 1 can show results in [timeframe], but true organizational integration typically takes [longer timeframe]. The key is delivering value continuously throughout the journey, not waiting for perfect implementation."
