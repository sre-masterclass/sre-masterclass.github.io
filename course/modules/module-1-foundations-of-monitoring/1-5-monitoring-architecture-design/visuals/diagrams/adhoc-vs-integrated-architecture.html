Monitoring Architecture: Ad-Hoc vs. Integrated

Ad-Hoc Monitoring Tools

Scattered, uncoordinated tools leading to data silos and operational blind spots.

Team A: Prometheus

Monitors service X, but data is not shared.

Team B: Datadog

Monitors service Y, with different alert rules.

Team C: ELK Stack

Manages logs, but not correlated with metrics.

Integrated Enterprise Architecture

A unified system that provides a single source of truth and enables cross-team collaboration.

Unified Monitoring Platform

All data (metrics, logs, traces) is collected, correlated, and visualized in one place.

  • Shared Dashboards
  • Standardized Alerting
  • Cross-Team Workflows
  • Business Objective Alignment
