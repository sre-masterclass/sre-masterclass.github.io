SRE Organizational Evolution Timeline

Stage 1: Early Stage

10-50 Engineers

Model: Centralized (1-3 SREs)

Focus: Basic monitoring, incident response, foundational automation.

Stage 2: Growth Stage

50-200 Engineers

Model: Expanding Centralized or early Embedded

Focus: Standardized practices, cross-team coordination, advanced tooling.

Stage 3: Scale Stage

200+ Engineers

Model: Hybrid (Platform + Embedded)

Focus: Org-wide reliability culture, advanced automation, strategic planning.
