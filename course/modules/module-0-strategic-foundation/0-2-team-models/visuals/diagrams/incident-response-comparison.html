Incident Response Comparison: Major Service Outage

Embedded Model

0-5 mins

Product team SRE immediately engaged. Deep service knowledge accelerates diagnosis.

5-15 mins

Team makes autonomous decisions on recovery and communication.

15-30 mins

Cross-squad coordination for related services. Shared platform tools enable rapid scaling.

Centralized Model

0-5 mins

Centralized monitoring detects issue across multiple services simultaneously.

5-15 mins

Platform SRE coordinates response across teams using standard procedures.

15-30 mins

Automated systems and shared tooling enable rapid mitigation and recovery.

Hybrid Model

0-5 mins

Embedded SRE handles service-specific issues. Platform team manages infrastructure.

5-15 mins

Escalation to platform team for complex infrastructure correlation and advanced tooling.

15-30 mins

Combined service expertise and platform capabilities enable comprehensive resolution.
