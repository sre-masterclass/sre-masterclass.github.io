## Visual Asset Specifications

### Module 0.1

*   **Executive Dashboard**:
    *   **Type**: Static Image/Slide
    *   **Content**: Bar chart showing downtime costs for Amazon, Netflix, Spotify.
    *   **Data Source**: Script references (Amazon: $66,240/min).
*   **Cost Calculation Dashboard**:
    *   **Type**: Interactive HTML/JavaScript
    *   **Content**: Real-time calculation of direct revenue impact and indirect business impact.
    *   **Features**: "Show Downtime Cost Calculator" button.
*   **ROI Framework Visualization**:
    *   **Type**: Static Image/Slide
    *   **Content**: Side-by-side comparison of Investment Required vs. Measurable Returns.
    *   **Features**: "Calculate Returns" button simulation.
