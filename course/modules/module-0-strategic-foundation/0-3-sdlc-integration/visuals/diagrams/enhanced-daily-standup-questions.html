Daily Standup: Traditional vs. SRE-Enhanced

Traditional Questions

  • What did you do yesterday?
  • What will you do today?
  • Any blockers?

SRE-Enhanced Questions

  • What did you do yesterday?
    Any reliability concerns with that work?
  • What will you do today?
    How does this impact our SLO targets?
  • Any blockers?
    Any monitoring or infrastructure blockers?
  • What's our current error budget status?
  • Any reliability risks in today's planned work?
