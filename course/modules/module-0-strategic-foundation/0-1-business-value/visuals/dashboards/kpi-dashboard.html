Indicator Definitions (Score 1-5)

  • Error Budget Health: A score of 5 means 0% of the error budget is burned. A score of 1 means >80% is burned. (Higher is better)
  • Deployment Success: A score based on the percentage of successful deployments (e.g., 5 for >99%, 1 for <90%).
  • SRE Maturity: A qualitative score from 1 (Ad-hoc) to 5 (Optimized) based on team practices.
