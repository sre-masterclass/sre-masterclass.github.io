Executive Communication Hierarchy

Level 1: CEO/C-Suite Engagement

CEO/Board Level Communication

  • Business Risk & Competitive Advantage
  • Customer Experience & Revenue Protection
  • Strategic Technology Investment ROI
Quarterly CEO Dashboard Example:
  • Revenue Protected: $2.3M this quarter
  • Customer Satisfaction: 15% improvement
  • Competitive Advantage: 99.95% uptime

Level 2: VP/Director Engagement

VP/Director Level Communication

  • Organizational Performance & Team Effectiveness
  • Strategic Initiative Progress & Resource Needs
  • Cross-Functional Collaboration Success
Monthly VP Dashboard Example:
  • Team Collaboration: 40% improvement
  • Resource Efficiency: 25% reduction in overhead
  • Strategic Progress: On track for 99.9% uptime

Level 3: Senior Manager/Team Lead Engagement

Manager Level Communication

  • Operational Excellence & Process Improvement
  • Team Development & Capability Building
  • Technical Implementation & Innovation
Weekly Manager Dashboard Example:
  • Process Improvement: 30% faster incident resolution
  • Team Development: 5 engineers certified
  • Innovation Impact: 20 hours/week saved
