Implementation Maturity Progression

Months 1-6: Strategic Foundation Implementation

  • Complete business case development with stakeholder alignment
  • Select and implement appropriate team model
  • Begin SDLC integration with pilot teams

Months 6-12: Cross-Functional Integration & Culture Development

  • Expand SDLC integration across all engineering teams
  • Implement cross-functional collaboration frameworks
  • Begin culture development initiatives

Months 12-24: Enterprise Scale & Sustainability Achievement

  • Achieve organization-wide SRE culture
  • Establish sustainable platform and community systems
  • Develop industry leadership and external recognition
