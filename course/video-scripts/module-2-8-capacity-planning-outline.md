# Module 2.8: Capacity Planning with SLO Integration - Content Outline

## Learning Objectives
- Understand SLO-driven capacity planning methodology for predictive system scaling, resource optimization, and business growth correlation with technical reliability requirements
- Master practical implementation of capacity planning systems that integrate SLO trend analysis, error budget burn rates, and predictive modeling for automated scaling decisions
- Explain complex relationships between SLO compliance trends, business growth patterns, capacity allocation strategies, and system performance under resource constraints
- Apply systematic capacity planning strategy to establish proactive resource management that aligns technical scaling with business objectives and cost optimization

## Template Selection
- **Chosen Template**: Integration Workshop Template
- **Rationale**: Students need to understand enterprise-scale capacity planning integration with CI/CD workflows, business planning cycles, and automated scaling systems
- **Duration**: 12-18 minutes (comprehensive integration workshop with predictive modeling, business correlation, and automated scaling implementation)
- **Chaos Integration**: `resource-starvation.yml` scenario at 8:00 mark to demonstrate capacity planning response during resource exhaustion and scaling decision-making

## Content Structure

### Introduction: Capacity Planning Reality vs Reactive Scaling (90 seconds)
- **Hook**: "Most organizations treat capacity planning as reactive firefighting, scaling resources after problems occur rather than predicting needs before constraints impact SLO compliance"
- **Context**: Traditional capacity planning vs SLO-driven predictive capacity management with business growth correlation
- **Problem Setup**: Show same business growth handled by reactive scaling vs predictive SLO-integrated capacity planning with cost and reliability differences
- **Learning Objective Preview**: Build understanding of proactive capacity planning that aligns technical scaling with business objectives and SLO compliance

### Part 1: SLO Trend Analysis & Predictive Capacity Modeling (3-4 minutes)
- **SLO Compliance Trend Analysis**: Historical SLO performance correlation with resource utilization patterns and business growth metrics
- **Predictive Capacity Modeling**: Using SLO burn rates, error budget consumption, and performance trend analysis for future resource requirement estimation
- **Business Growth Correlation**: Aligning capacity planning with business forecasts, seasonal patterns, and strategic growth initiatives
- **Cost Optimization Integration**: Balancing SLO compliance requirements with infrastructure cost management and resource efficiency optimization

### Part 2: Automated Scaling Integration & SLO Threshold Management (3-4 minutes)
- **SLO-Driven Auto-scaling**: Implementing scaling triggers based on SLO compliance trends rather than simple resource utilization metrics
- **Multi-Window Scaling Logic**: Using burn rate calculations and error budget velocity for intelligent scaling decision-making
- **Capacity Buffer Management**: Maintaining SLO compliance margins while optimizing resource allocation and cost efficiency
- **Integration Workflow**: CI/CD pipeline integration for capacity planning automation and deployment coordination

### Part 3: Resource Starvation Response & Scaling Validation (2-3 minutes)
- **Baseline Capacity Planning**: Normal operation showing predictive scaling and SLO-driven resource allocation
- **Resource Starvation Scenario**: Simulated resource exhaustion testing capacity planning response and automated scaling effectiveness
- **Scaling Decision Analysis**: Real-time capacity planning response with SLO compliance protection and business impact minimization
- **Performance Validation**: Measuring capacity planning effectiveness through SLO compliance maintenance and resource optimization metrics

### Part 4: Business Integration & Long-term Capacity Strategy (2-3 minutes)
- **Business Planning Integration**: Capacity planning coordination with quarterly business planning, product roadmaps, and growth projections
- **Strategic Capacity Investment**: Long-term infrastructure planning based on SLO compliance requirements and business strategic objectives
- **Cost-Performance Optimization**: Balancing SLO compliance with infrastructure budget management and resource efficiency strategies
- **Continuous Improvement**: Capacity planning refinement based on SLO trend analysis, business feedback, and operational learning

### Part 5: Enterprise Workflow Integration & Automation (1-2 minutes)
- **CI/CD Pipeline Integration**: Automated capacity planning as part of deployment workflows and release management processes
- **Business Stakeholder Communication**: Executive reporting on capacity planning effectiveness, cost optimization, and SLO compliance trends
- **Organizational Coordination**: Cross-team capacity planning coordination with product, finance, and infrastructure teams
- **Next Steps**: Advanced monitoring and anomaly detection integration for predictive capacity intelligence
