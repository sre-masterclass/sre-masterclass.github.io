# Module 2.1: SLO Definition Workshop & Stakeholder Alignment - Content Outline

## Learning Objectives
- Understand SLO business value proposition and its measurable organizational impact on customer satisfaction and revenue
- Master collaborative SLO definition frameworks for implementing effective stakeholder alignment across product, engineering, and business teams
- Evaluate organizational trade-offs using data-driven SLO decision frameworks that balance reliability investment with feature velocity
- Apply cross-functional collaboration patterns to build effective SLO consensus that drives both technical excellence and business outcomes

## Template Selection
- **Chosen Template**: Strategic Foundation Template
- **Rationale**: Students need business and organizational foundation before diving into technical SLI implementation and error budget mathematics
- **Duration**: 8-10 minutes (strategic and business-focused with stakeholder engagement patterns)
- **Chaos Integration**: None needed (business-focused content, stakeholder alignment focus)

## Content Structure

### Introduction: The Business Case for Collaborative SLO Definition (90 seconds)
- **Hook**: "SLOs that engineering loves but business ignores are just expensive monitoring - and SLOs that business demands but engineering can't deliver destroy team morale"
- **Context**: Business vs engineering tension in reliability investment vs feature velocity trade-offs
- **Problem Setup**: Show common SLO definition failures (too aggressive, too loose, misaligned stakeholders)
- **Learning Objective Preview**: Build collaborative SLO definition process that aligns business value with technical reality

### Part 1: Business Value & SLO ROI Framework (2.5-3 minutes)
- **Hidden Cost of Poor Reliability**: Customer churn, support escalation, engineering opportunity cost analysis
- **SLO Business Value Quantification**: Revenue protection, customer satisfaction correlation, competitive advantage measurement
- **ROI Framework for Reliability Investment**: Engineering time vs business impact calculation, reliability vs velocity trade-off optimization
- **Industry Benchmarks**: SLO success patterns from Netflix, Google, Amazon showing business outcomes

### Part 2: Stakeholder Alignment & Collaboration Models (2.5-3 minutes)
- **Cross-Functional SLO Team Structure**: Product managers, engineers, business stakeholders, customer support integration
- **SLO Definition Workshop Framework**: Systematic process for collaborative SLO creation with conflict resolution
- **Business-Technical Translation Patterns**: Converting customer experience requirements into measurable technical SLOs
- **Stakeholder Communication Strategy**: Different SLO perspectives for executives, product teams, engineering teams, operations teams

### Part 3: SLO Implementation Strategy & Change Management (2-2.5 minutes)
- **Phased SLO Rollout Roadmap**: Pilot services → critical path → full organization with learning milestones
- **SLO Governance Framework**: Regular review cycles, stakeholder feedback loops, SLO evolution management
- **Organizational Readiness Assessment**: Technical capability, cultural readiness, stakeholder engagement maturity
- **Change Management for SLO Culture**: From reactive firefighting to proactive reliability management

### Part 4: Success Metrics & Continuous Stakeholder Engagement (90 seconds)
- **Business Impact Metrics**: Customer satisfaction correlation, revenue protection measurement, competitive advantage tracking
- **Stakeholder Satisfaction Metrics**: Engineering velocity, product delivery confidence, business predictability improvement
- **SLO Effectiveness Measurement**: Alert fatigue reduction, incident response improvement, customer experience enhancement
- **Long-term SLO Evolution**: Organizational learning patterns, stakeholder maturity development, business value amplification
